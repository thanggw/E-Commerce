Account Questions

1. How do I register an account?
To register an account, you need to: - Click the "Register" button at the top right corner - Fill in all personal information - Confirm your registered email - Complete the registration process
2. How do I recover my password?
If you forget your password, you can: - Click on "Forgot Password?" on the login page - Enter your registered email - Follow the instructions in the recovery email
3. How do I change my personal information?
To update your personal information: - Log in to your account - Go to "Account Information" - Select "Edit Information" - Update and save changes
4. How do I delete my account?
To request account deletion: - Log in to your account - Go to "Account Settings" - Select "Request Account Deletion" - Confirm via email Note: This process cannot be undone and will delete all your order history.

Ordering Questions

1. How do I place an order on the website?
The ordering process includes the following steps: - Select a product and add it to the cart - Check the cart and quantities - Fill in the shipping information - Choose the payment method - Confirm the order
2. How do I track my order?
You can track your order by: - Logging into your account - Going to "My Orders" - Clicking on the order code to see details - Or entering the tracking code in the tracking tool
3. How do I cancel my order?
To cancel an order: - Log in and go to "My Orders" - Select the order you want to cancel - Click "Cancel Order" and select a reason Note: Orders that are not processed or are preparing items can only be canceled.
4. Is there a limit on order quantities?
Regarding order limits: - There is no limit on the number of orders - Some products may have quantity limits per order - Bulk orders may receive special discounts - Contact customer service for advice on bulk orders

Payment Questions

1. What payment methods are accepted?
We accept the following payment methods: - Credit/Debit cards (Visa, Mastercard, JCB) - Bank transfers - E-wallets (Momo, ZaloPay, VNPay) - Cash on delivery (COD) - Installments through financial partners
2. How do I receive a VAT invoice?
To receive a VAT invoice: - Select the "Issue VAT Invoice" option when placing an order - Fill in the company's information - The invoice will be sent via email after the order is completed
3. What is the refund policy?
The refund process: - Refund within 7-14 business days - Refund to the original payment method - For COD, refund via bank transfer - Provide account information through the refund form
4. How do I apply a discount code?
How to use a discount code: - Enter the code in the "Discount Code" box on the cart page - One order can only apply one code - Check the conditions for each code - Discount codes do not apply to shipping fees

Shipping Questions

1. How long does shipping take?
Estimated shipping times: - Within the city: 1-2 days - Nearby provinces: 2-3 days - Distant provinces: 3-5 days - Times may vary during peak seasons
2. How is the shipping fee calculated?
Shipping fees are calculated based on: - Distance of delivery - Weight of the order - Free for orders over 500,000 VND - Extra charge for express delivery
3. Is delivery available on weekends?
About weekend delivery: - Delivery is available on Saturday and Sunday - Applies to urban areas - You can choose your preferred delivery time - Some areas may not support weekend delivery
4. What is the return/exchange policy?
Return/exchange policy: - Free returns/exchanges within 7 days - Products must be in original tags - Must have a purchase invoice - The company covers return shipping fees if the product is faulty - Customers bear return shipping fees if the return is based on personal choice
